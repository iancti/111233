TEAM CYTO

ABOUT CYTO

CYTO- The basic unit and function of biological structure, the smallest unit of a living being with complete vitality.
It symbolizes that everyone can stimulate every potential of the cell in daily training or competition.

Established an overall team brand image with a new design and is active in the cycling world in Taiwan.
Besides competitions, we actively cultivate social marketing, and holds independent riding activities and competitions to promote cycling.
